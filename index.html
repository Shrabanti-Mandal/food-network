About Us

Khana's is the first Bangladeshi Restaurant chain that specializes in fast food, such as Naga Wings Barrel, Potato Wedges, and peri-peri style chicken dishes etc with cold-coffee.Khana’s is a Bangladeshi fast-food company, founded on 14th April 2015 as a restaurant operated by Ehsan Ahmed Jaysun, in Bashundhara, Dhaka, Bangladesh. He rechristened his business as a Burger stand named as Ande Khana in the year 2012, and later turned the company into a franchise, with the new name Khana’s being introduced in 2015 at a location in Dhaka. Khana’s has its original headquarters in Bashundhara, Dhaka called Club House Branch.

My Special cold-coffee recipies

Make a 200ml cup of black coffee following pack instructions, then allow the coffee to go completely cold. Pour into a blender with the milk along with 2 or 3 handfuls of ice and maple syrup, if using, then blend until smooth and foamy. Pour into a chilled tall glass and serve.
